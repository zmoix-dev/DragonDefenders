Dragon's Defense
	Player Experience: Tactical/Strategic
	
	Core Mechanic: Using limited resources, defend your base from waves of enemies.
	
	Premise: Dragon uses traps and minions to defend his newly usurped village.
	
	Style: Medieval low-poly
	
	Platform: WebGL
	
	Requirements:
		Player places obstacles.
		Enemies walk a path towards a goal through those obstacles
	
	Stretch Goals:
		Dynamic enemy pathfinding
		Construct build timer
